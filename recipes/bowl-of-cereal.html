Bowl of Cereal

A childhood classic. We all know and love this recipe.


Ingredients

you will need

  • cereal of choice
  • milk

Recipe

  1. find a bowl. for an extra bit of flair, wash the bowl beforehand.
  2. grab your cereal of choice and pour contents into the bowl.
  3. grab you milk. shake it up and pour atop the Cereal making sure to douse as much of the surface as possible. Stop pouring before the milk envelopes the cereal.
  4. grab spoon and eat. you have 10 minutes to finish before it is spoiled.
